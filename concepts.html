false positive etc.

false positive（FP）: indicates presence of a condition when in reality it is not present - 误诊为有病

false negative（FN）: indicates no presence of a condition (the result is negative), when in reality it is present - 误诊为无病

true positive（TP）: 正确的判定为有病

true negative（TN）: 正确的判定为无病

mAP

IoU

Intersection over Union is a ratio between the intersection and the union of the predicted boxes and the ground truth boxes.

Precision and Recall

Precision = TP/(TP+FP)
Recall = TP/(TP+FN)

mAP

The AP (average precision) is defined as the mean of the Precision values at these chosen N Recall values.

The mAP hence is the Mean of all the Average Precision values across all your classes
